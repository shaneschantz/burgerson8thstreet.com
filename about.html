Burgers on 8th Street is a design creative based in Billings, Montana founded and run solo by Shane Schantz. Burgers on 8th Street takes a local and unique approach with graphic design to make your business pop!

Services I can provide include event designs from t-shirts, banners, posters, and much more. I also love establishing a modern and minimal brand identy to companys pre-existing or completely new.
